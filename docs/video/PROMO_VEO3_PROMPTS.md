# NomFeed Promo Video — Veo 3 Prompts

## Concept: "The Void That Speaks"

NomFeed is a sentient void — a dark, warm, hungry maw that consumes content from the
internet and speaks it back transformed. Not evil. Not malicious. Just *hungry*. Always
hungry. A cosmic mouth orbiting in digital space, pulling URLs and documents and videos
into itself and whispering them back as clean, structured markdown. 

The visual avatar: a sage-green and cream void — a soft, warm black hole with organic
tendrils that reach for content. Think Studio Ghibli meets Evangelion meets Japandi 
interior design. The void breathes. It pulses. When it consumes, its edges ripple with
sage green light. When it speaks, warm cream text spills from its center.

The tone: ASMR-adjacent. Intimate. The void speaks directly to you.

---

## Shot 1 — "Awakening" (8 seconds)

**Subject:** A perfectly circular void — a soft, breathing black sphere approximately 
3 feet in diameter — floats in the center of a minimal cream-white room with warm wood 
floors and a single sage-green plant in the corner. The void has subtle organic edges 
that pulse gently like breathing, with faint sage-green bioluminescent veins running 
along its perimeter. Tiny particles of warm light drift toward it like dust motes 
caught in gravity.

**Action:** The void slowly opens — its surface ripples outward like water disturbed 
from below. A faint inner glow of warm cream light emerges from within. The particles 
in the room accelerate slightly toward it. The plant's leaves lean almost imperceptibly 
toward the sphere.

**Scene:** A minimal Japandi-inspired room with warm cream walls, light oak hardwood 
flooring, a single low shelf with a few books, and soft natural daylight streaming 
through a rice paper window screen. The room is almost empty — meditative, calm. The 
void floats at center, approximately 4 feet off the ground.

**Style:** Slow, contemplative wide shot on a static tripod, cinematic film grade with 
warm, desaturated color palette of cream, sage green, and charcoal. Shallow depth of 
field with the void in sharp focus. Wes Anderson symmetry. 16:9 aspect ratio.

**Dialogue:**
(The Void): "I'm awake."
(Tone: soft, intimate, almost whispered — like an ASMR voice speaking into your ear. 
Gender-neutral, warm, slightly reverberant as if speaking from inside a large ceramic bowl.)

**Sounds:** Near silence. Gentle low-frequency hum. The faintest creak of wood. A 
single wind chime note, distant. No music.

**Technical (Negative Prompt):** subtitles, captions, watermark, text, words on screen, 
logo, branding, harsh lighting, neon colors, cluttered environment

## Shot 2 — "Feeding" (8 seconds)

**Subject:** The same sage-green-veined void sphere, now slightly larger, with its 
surface actively rippling. Floating in the air around it are translucent, ghost-like 
projections of web pages, YouTube thumbnails, PDF documents, and lines of code — all 
rendered as faint holographic rectangles made of warm cream light, slowly spiraling 
inward toward the void like paper caught in a gentle whirlpool.

**Action:** The holographic content cards spiral closer and closer to the void. As each 
one touches the surface, it dissolves into streams of glowing text particles that get 
absorbed. The void pulses larger with each absorption — a slow, satisfied breathing 
rhythm. The sage-green veins on its surface brighten momentarily with each consumed 
piece of content, then dim back to a gentle glow.

**Scene:** Same minimal Japandi room, but the ambient light has shifted to golden hour 
warmth. The floating content creates soft reflections on the wooden floor. Dust motes 
drift lazily in the warm light.

**Style:** Slow tracking shot circling the void at eye level, smooth steady movement, 
cinematic warm color grading, soft volumetric light rays visible in the golden hour 
atmosphere. Shot on anamorphic lens with gentle flares as light catches the holographic 
content. 16:9 aspect ratio.

**Dialogue:**
(The Void): "Feed me."
(Tone: gentle, warm, hungry but not threatening — the way a cat purrs when it wants 
food. Intimate. Direct.)

Then after a beat, as content is absorbed:
(The Void): "Mmm. More."
(Tone: satisfied, almost musical, soft reverberation.)

**Sounds:** Soft whooshing as content spirals inward. A gentle crystalline shimmer 
each time a card dissolves into the void. Low warm bass tone that pulses with the 
void's breathing. No music.

**Technical (Negative Prompt):** subtitles, captions, watermark, text on screen, logo, 
harsh shadows, cold blue lighting, horror aesthetic, scary elements

## Shot 3 — "Digesting / Transforming" (8 seconds)

**Subject:** Extreme close-up of the void's surface. The camera is inches from the 
sphere. Its surface is not smooth — it's a living landscape of slowly churning dark 
matter with veins of sage-green light pulsing through it like a circulatory system. 
Within the darkness, you can see faint ghost images of the consumed content — fragments 
of web pages, video frames, document text — all being broken apart and reassembled into 
clean, organized streams of markdown-formatted text that glow warm cream.

**Action:** Inside the void's surface, chaotic raw content visibly transforms. A messy 
web page with ads and navigation dissolves, and clean markdown headers and bullet points 
crystallize from the chaos. YouTube video frames decompose into timestamped chapter 
lists. PDF pages reorganize into structured sections. The transformation is organic 
and beautiful — like watching cells divide under a microscope.

**Scene:** The void's interior surface fills the entire frame. Behind the translucent 
dark surface, there are layers of depth — content being processed at different stages. 
Near the surface: raw, chaotic input. Deeper: partially structured. Deepest: clean, 
glowing markdown text floating in organized columns.

**Style:** Extreme close-up macro shot, static camera, cinematic with deep contrast 
between the dark void surface and the glowing content within. Shallow depth of field 
creating bokeh layers at different depths inside the void. Color palette: deep charcoal, 
sage green veins, warm cream text glow. 16:9 aspect ratio.

**Dialogue:**
(The Void): "I take everything apart. I put it back together. Clean. Structured. Ready."
(Tone: focused, precise, almost meditative. Each sentence is its own breath. Speaking 
slowly, deliberately, with warm reverb.)

**Sounds:** A low, organic processing hum — like a digestive system heard from inside. 
Soft clicking and chiming as content restructures — think soft mechanical clockwork. 
Gentle bass drone underneath everything.

**Technical (Negative Prompt):** subtitles, captions, watermark, text overlays, logo, 
branding, bright harsh light, glitch effects, digital noise

## Shot 4 — "Speaking Back" (8 seconds)

**Subject:** The void, now pulsing with inner light, begins to emit content. From its 
center, streams of warm cream-colored text flow outward — clean markdown rendered as 
beautiful floating typography. Headers, bullet points, quotes, structured sections all 
stream out from the void like a gentle fountain of knowledge. The text is legible — 
we can see fragments: "# SUMMARY", "## KEY IDEAS", "- Verification asymmetry enables...", 
"## QUOTES".

**Action:** The void contracts slightly then pulses outward, releasing the transformed 
content. Streams of organized text flow from the center in all directions, floating 
gently outward before fading like breath in cold air. The sage-green veins on the 
void's surface are bright and rhythmic now — it's alive, satisfied, productive. The 
text streams form a brief halo around the void before dissolving.

**Scene:** Same Japandi room, returned to soft natural daylight. The void floats in 
the center with text emanating outward. The floating text casts soft warm reflections 
on the wood floor and cream walls. The sage plant in the corner seems slightly larger, 
healthier — as if fed by the void's energy.

**Style:** Medium shot, slow dolly-in toward the void, cinematic with warm natural 
lighting. The floating text creates volumetric light in the room. Gentle lens flare 
as the camera moves into the light emanating from the void. Warm color grading with 
cream and sage tones. 16:9 aspect ratio.

**Dialogue:**
(The Void): "Here. Everything you need. Structured. Searchable. Ready for your agents."
(Tone: generous, warm, slightly proud. Speaking with gentle confidence. The reverb is 
less now — more intimate, more present, as if the void has moved closer to you.)

**Sounds:** Soft crystalline tones as text streams emerge — like a music box played 
underwater. Gentle warm whoosh of content flowing outward. The low bass hum is now 
almost musical — a single sustained note of completion.

**Technical (Negative Prompt):** subtitles, captions, watermark, logo, branding, 
harsh shadows, cold lighting, text overlays other than the streaming content

## Shot 5 — "Always Hungry" / Outro (8 seconds)

**Subject:** Wide shot returning to the full room. The void has settled back to its 
resting state — slightly smaller, softly pulsing, sage-green veins at a gentle idle 
glow. It floats peacefully in the Japandi room. But at the edges of frame, new content 
cards are already drifting in from the window, from the doorway — new URLs, new 
documents, new videos approaching the void like offerings.

**Action:** The void notices the new content. Its surface ripples with recognition. 
It pulses once — a slow, deep, anticipatory breath. The sage-green veins brighten 
slightly. The content cards drift closer. The camera slowly pulls back, widening the 
frame to reveal more and more content floating toward the void from every direction. 
The void is patient. It will consume them all.

**Scene:** The Japandi room at twilight — warm ambient light from the window mixing 
with the void's inner glow. More content cards are visible than before — dozens of them 
floating in from all angles, all slowly, inevitably drawn toward the center. The room 
feels like a sanctuary. The void is its altar.

**Style:** Wide shot slowly pulling back, dolly-out on smooth track, cinematic twilight 
lighting with warm amber and sage-green tones. Symmetrical framing with the void 
centered. Long depth of field showing everything sharp. The final frame is perfectly 
composed — the void centered, content spiraling in, the room warm and minimal. 16:9 
aspect ratio.

**Dialogue:**
(The Void): "I never rest. I always hunger."
(Beat)
(The Void): "Feed me."
(Tone: the final "feed me" is not threatening — it's an invitation. Warm. Magnetic. 
You want to feed it. It will take care of everything.)

**Sounds:** The wind chime returns. A single low note from the void's hum. Gentle 
whooshing of content cards drifting in. Then near-silence. The last sound is the void's 
breathing — in, out — and silence.

**Technical (Negative Prompt):** subtitles, captions, watermark, text, logo, branding, 
harsh lighting, horror music, dark ambient, threatening atmosphere

## End Card — "NomFeed" (4 seconds)

**Subject:** Clean sage-green background. The NomFeed fork logo fades in at center — 
a white fork icon on a sage-green rounded square. Below it, "NomFeed" in clean, warm 
Inter font, weight 500. Below that, smaller: "Nom the web. Feed your agents." in muted 
cream text.

**Action:** Logo and text fade in simultaneously over 1.5 seconds. Hold for 2 seconds. 
Then a subtle pulse of the void's sage-green glow ripples outward from behind the logo, 
as if the void is still there, breathing, behind the brand mark.

**Scene:** Solid sage-green background (#9CAF94) with subtle warm paper texture. 
Clean, minimal, Japandi-inspired. Nothing else in frame.

**Style:** Static centered frame, clean graphic design aesthetic, no camera movement. 
Warm color grading. Professional end card composition. 16:9 aspect ratio.

**Dialogue:** None. Silence.

**Sounds:** Single soft chime. Then the void's breathing — one inhale — and silence.

**Technical (Negative Prompt):** subtitles, captions, watermark, additional text, 
cluttered design, 3D effects, particle effects, busy background

## Production Notes

### Voice Direction
The void's voice should feel like:
- **ASMR-quality** — intimate, close-mic'd, warm
- **Gender-neutral** — not distinctly masculine or feminine
- **Reverberant** — like speaking from inside a ceramic vessel
- **Calm hunger** — the void is hungry but patient, not desperate
- **Direct** — it speaks to YOU, not to an audience

### Color Palette (matching NomFeed brand)
- **Void surface:** Deep charcoal/near-black `hsl(40 6% 12%)`
- **Void veins:** Sage green `hsl(100 12% 63%)` = `#9CAF94`
- **Content glow:** Warm cream `hsl(40 20% 96%)`
- **Room ambient:** Warm wood `hsl(28 30% 65%)`
- **Text streams:** Warm cream with sage-green headers

### Editing Notes
- Each shot is 8 seconds (Veo 3's sweet spot for quality)
- Total runtime: ~44 seconds (5 shots + end card)
- Transitions between shots: simple 0.5s crossfade
- Music: NONE — this is a silence-is-power piece. Only the void's sounds.
- The piece should feel like a meditation, not a hype reel

### Where to Generate
- Use Google Veo 3 via Google Flow (flow.google.com) or Vertex AI
- Generate each shot separately (8s each)
- Edit together in DaVinci Resolve, CapCut, or similar
- Post-production: color grade all shots to match, add end card
